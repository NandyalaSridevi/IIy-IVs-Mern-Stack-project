CraveKart frontend needs a local server.

To open it correctly on Windows:
1. Double-click start-frontend.bat
2. Wait until it says the server started on http://localhost:8000
3. Open http://localhost:8000 in your browser

Admin page:
http://localhost:8000/admin.html

If you open a saved browser shortcut before starting the server,
you may see "This site can't be reached".
